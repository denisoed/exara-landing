Frequently Asked Questions

Go to the extension store of your browser (e.g. Chrome Web Store) and click "Install". After installation, the Exara icon will appear on the browser toolbar.

Currently, we support Google Chrome and browsers based on it (Yandex.Browser, Edge, Brave). We are working on supporting Firefox and Safari.

Each time you highlight text and get an AI-explanation, this counts as one request. The clarifying question to the same explanation does not consume additional requests. The "Ask AI anything" and "Summarize" functions (when available) will also consume requests.

We take data privacy seriously. Exara only processes the text you highlight to generate an explanation and does not collect your browsing history or personal data. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
